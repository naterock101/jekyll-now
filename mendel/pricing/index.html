Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

Got a question? We've got answers. If you have some other questions, contact us using email at support@mendelsensors.com. (Ask for Mike. Tell him Eric sent you.)

How much does the unit cost?

We charge $20 for our sensors and it comes with the sensor plus three free months of service. After that we charge you on a monthly basis. If you decide to stop using our service you don't pay us. But we think you'll enjoy using it.

Can you explain the monthly fee?

We make money by charging you a monthly fee. We think this aligns us all towards the same goals. It's cheap for you to try and keeps us continuing to reinvest in making our platform better.

Why can't I set up payments on the iOS app?

Unfortunately, Apple takes a 30% commission! That's a really high number and would ential us increasing our price by 50%! We think this way is better for you.

Is this a secure site for purchases?

Absolutely! We work with Stripe which guarantees your safety and security. All billing information is stored on our payment processing partner which has the most stringent level of certification available in the payments industry.

Can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime in your account. Once the subscription is cancelled, you will not be charged next month. You will continue to have access to your account until your current subscription expires.

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes! We totally get that you might have a few months inbetween grows where you don't need to keep paying us but want your data to still be available in the future.

Can I update my card details?

Yes. Go to the billing section of your dashboard and update your payment information.

Can I request refund?

Unfortunately, not. We do not issue full or partial refunds for any reason.

Can I try your service for free?

When you purchase a unit it comes with three free months of service!
